Jönköping

What to do?

Jönköping is a versatile city, fit for the hiking nature lover and active city tourist. Visit the match museum
and get a new perspective on the small everyday item with a surprising backstory. Or, take a stroll along
Vätternstranden and breathe in the fresh air. It does not matter the season, this activity will always be a treat.

Where to go?

If you want to experience the nightlife of Jönköping? Lucky for you, there are many places to go.
Bongo bar is a good alternative for those who are in search of many options in one place. Why not try their
three B burger or order some snacs to your drink. Join them on different events such as Bongo Live or just go
to the dance floor; always a classic.

What to eat?

Deciding where to eat can be a jungle if you are new to town. For those who feel lost Zaffran is the way to go.
Their classic persian food is bound to satisfy your cravings and you might find a new favourite.
